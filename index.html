Welcome.......

Matley Legal is predominantly an Executive Search (Head-hunting) organisation with a focus on Partner-and Senior Associate level recruitment for leading UK Regional, National and International law firms.

A law graduate subsequently spending many years' working in the legal profession.  Also a qualified Member of the International Compliance Association.

I have been recruiting into the legal profession since 2005, have strong working relationships with a number of high profile lawyers and am well connected to senior decision makers including Managing Partners, Heads of Department, and HR Directors.  Highly experienced in Search & Selection and Contingency recruitment I pride myself on my industry knowledge and Search success. 

I have never liked the sales approach to recruitment and aim to guide my candidates through this often difficult process helping them make the right long term career choice.  Determined to set up a consultancy which does more than simply act as a middleman passing on CVs from candidate to client, I provide a more personal, tailor-made, bespoke and thorough recruitment service and offer my considerable skills and market insight to Partners and Senior Associates across all disciplines.  Clients and candidates alike praise me for my empathetic approach.  Confidentiality and discretion is extremely important to me.  I am well known for my integrity as a head-hunter and as such candidates know when they are contacted by me there is a genuine opportunity for them to consider.

The easiest way to contact me is to call either the office or my mobile number or send an email, details of which are on the website.  Please note this applies in or out of office hours, as I know it is sometimes difficult for lawyers to make time during the day.  Alternatively please fill in the contact form below and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

Any contact will be treated in the utmost confidence.  Thank you.
